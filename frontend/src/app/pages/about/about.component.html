About us

Who We Are

We are a team of developers and enthusiasts for modern digital solutions who believe that vacation planning should not be stressful. Our goal is to bring a simple, secure and reliable way for employees to manage their time off, and for employers to efficiently track requests and plan capacities with a clear overview.

Our Vision

We believe that organisations are successful when they truly listen to people – both those on the front line and those in the offices. That is why we created Timora Vacation Planner: to make communication between employees and managers easier and to simplify the entire vacation approval process.

How We Work

We work in short development sprints and closely cooperate with our users. New features are tested in real use as soon as possible while keeping the whole system stable. A modern CI/CD pipeline and a strong focus on code quality are a natural part of our process.

What We Value

Simplicity – an intuitive environment for everyone.

Security – reliable internal mechanisms and strong data protection.

Collaboration – ongoing dialogue with customers and end users.

Growth – continuous improvement and looking for new possibilities.
