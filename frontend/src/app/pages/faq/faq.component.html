FAQ

How can I book my vacation?

  1. Open the application and sign in with your company account.
  2. In the calendar, click the days you want to mark as vacation.
  3. Select the type of vacation (for example regular leave, home office, doctor, sick leave, other).
  4. Submit your request and wait for it to be approved by your manager.

Who approves my request?

Your direct manager is always responsible for approving your vacation. You will receive a notification about the status of your request by e‑mail or directly in the app.

Can I change or cancel my reservation?

Yes, as long as it has not been finally approved yet. In that case you can edit your request or ask your manager to cancel it.

Can my team see my vacation?

Yes, the team overview shows a list of all approved vacations. This helps colleagues plan their work and cover for each other in advance.

Will I receive a reminder before my vacation?

Yes. A few days before your vacation starts you will receive an e‑mail and/or a notification in the app so that you do not forget about your time off.

What if I cannot sign in?

First check that you are using the correct company login details. If the problem persists, contact your IT department or reach out to us at it-support@firma.sk.
